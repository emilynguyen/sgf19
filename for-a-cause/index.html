Sun God For A Cause

Every year, ASCE presents a new Sun God For a Cause project to give back to the local San Diego community. This year, ASCE has decided to stay on campus and partner with our friends at Healing Hands to bring you the first ever Sun God For A Cause Clothing Drive! Donate clothing items, such as jackets, beanies, blankets, or any other clothing item you do not use throughout week 2 (April 8 - 12) and help the homeless community around San Diego! Check out our Facebook Page for more info on how you can get involved!
